Fungeons

I was one of the three designers and developers of Fungeons, our Android app that is soon to hit the appstore. It features a player traversing any of six endless dungeons using a bow that creates platforms in walls to attempt to escape the invetiable void chasing them. It features not only single player but a multiplayer system where you challenge people in real time to see who can survive the fungeon longest. I was heavily involved in the design, GUI, and front end.

Fungeons was initially a small project to test out android programming, and to experience android studio and libgdx coding. Though during development it became a passion project, and became much more than we initially planned

We worked together both in person, and using Github to communicate and update the project together.

I had the oppourtunity to create a Sumobot for a competition. The competition was to knock an opponent out of a black board with white outline, it was coded so that if light sensors facing the ground would find white, it would turn back and once more try to find an opponent.

Finding the opponent was done using a frontal light sensor, which checked for changes, and would tell the bot to charge
